Pet Rescue Planner:

Pet Rescue Planner offers a fun and easy-to-use interface, built on top of the largest adoptable pet database in the United States. Our goal is to make finding the perfect pet to rescue as easy as possible, both for our users and for the 300,000 + pets we represent. Please leave a review if you find the app helpful!

Rut Tools:

Rut is a tool used by multinational mining companies to reduce fatalities and critical risks. By forcing miners to stop and think of dangers before they begin working, Rut Tools helps everyone stay aware and stay safe. Rut Tools uses technologies such as CoreData, CoreLocation and MapKit, Firebase database and authentication as well as creating unique user experiences for different levels of users such as admins and super admins.
